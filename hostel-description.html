The Oxley Residence

Located in perfectly sculpted landscapes lies the Oxley Residence; luxurious yet affordable student residences.

Most rooms in Oxley Residence have a wash and basin and double beds in some of the larger standard rooms. There are also 12 blocks, en suite with junior double beds.

Oxley House has a study area with computers, tables and sofas. Other facilities include a bar, common room, pool room, laundry facilities and cycle storage, a gym and fitness suite, music practice room and a community food growing project for students.

Oxley Residence is linked to the campus by a frequent bus service running till 3am. It’s also a 20 minutes’ walk to Headingley, one of Leeds’ popular student suburbs.
